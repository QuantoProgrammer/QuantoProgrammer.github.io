ABOUT US

Today's world has become fast and monotonous. Have you ever imagined a cupcake which actually takes 45 minutes to bake, takes only 5 minutes at a bakery to get served.

BOULANGERIE is a small startup by Maitri and Ripa Dadhaniya which showcases their skills and love for bakery. Everything is handmade, keeping in mind their customer's health and safety by serving freshly baked goodies.
